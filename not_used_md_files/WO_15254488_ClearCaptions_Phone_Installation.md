### ⚠️ IMPORTANT NOTICE
**PLEASE REVIEW THE WORK ORDER IN FULL PRIOR TO ACCEPTING**

All ClearCaptions Work Orders are pre-arranged with our customers and should only be requested and accepted by field technicians who are able to service as dated. ClearCaptions' scheduling accounts for customers' availability and the earliest available phone kit delivery date to ensure as minimal wait time as possible for our high-need population.

### OVERVIEW
This is a residential installation of a ClearCaptions captioning telephone (basic phone install) with a customer experiencing hearing loss. This job should take approximately 1-2 hours.

- The phone kit will already be on site
- The kit does NOT include a phone line splitter, DSL filters, phone line couplers, RJ-11 cables, or RJ-45 cables which are necessary to have on hand
- Information regarding ClearCaptions' payment structure can be found within Documents under "Work Order Payment Policy"
- Information regarding contacting customers and Clear Captions' rescheduling protocol can be found within Documents under "Customer Contact and Installation Reschedule Policy"

### REQUIRED TOOLS
- Line Splitter
- Couplers (RJ-11 Female to Female Extension Cord Coupler recommended)
- RJ-11 and CAT6 RJ-45 Cables (10'-12' recommended)
- Extension Cord (tight space model recommended)
- DSL Filter
