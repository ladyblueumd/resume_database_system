### 1. Site Arrival and Equipment Collection
**Arrive onsite and check in with the store personnel** - Obtain the UPS device that was shipped from Spencer Technologies (from) Medway MA.

### 2. Setup Preparation
**Unpackage the new UPS** at the network location (where old UPS currently is) but **DO NOT shut down the existing UPS yet.**

### 3. Spencer Bridge Check-in
**Call Spencer Bridge to check in**

**Dial in by phone:** 469-998-6065 ID: 611270310#  
**One touch dial:** +1 469-998-6065,,611270310#

### 4. Guitar Center Helpdesk Coordination
**Call the Guitar Center Helpdesk at (800)424-8888.**

They will work with you to **gracefully shut down equipment before unplugging UPS.**  
**Document the name of the agent** - you'll provide this to Spencer during check out.

### 5. UPS Replacement
**Swap out the UPS.** Power up UPS. Plug equipment back into UPS and boot up.

### 6. System Testing
**Test the equipment with the onsite store personnel** to ensure store is back online. **Document the name of the store personnel** you spoke to - provide this to Spencer during check out.

### 7. Old Equipment Disposal
**Package the old equipment** into the box the new UPS came in. **Provide to store personnel.** We do not take this with us.

### 8. Documentation
**Take a photo of the newly installed UPS.**

### 9. Final Deliverables
**Send deliverables to Spencer at GuitarCenterUPS@Spencertech.com**

**Includes:**
- Photo of the UPS installed with power lights showing and cables neat
- Name of the Network Team personnel
- Name of store personnel who tested equipment
