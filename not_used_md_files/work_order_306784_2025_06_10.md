This work order is for a router upgrade. Technician will be responsible for replacing a NetGate router with a new FortiGate router. Technician MUST arrive at Home Depot at exactly 9:30 p.m on the scheduled date. due to strict Home Depot policy. Check the public AT&T wireless on your laptop and verify that this is functioning properly. Once the Home Depot manager closes out their registers, the technician will call for verification to continue with router replacement. Technician will then take pictures of existing NetGate setup (front and back with visible link lights). The technician will have a network diagram to follow and an IDG attached to this WO) with step by step process. The actual physical process to replace the routers should only take about 20-30 minutes. Additional time will be necessary for check in and check out calls and possible router configuration troubleshooting. Once the routers are changed out, technician will call Home Depot IT and verify that the internal network is working and also call At&T to verify that they can see the new router. Then, the technician will check public AT&T wireless again. Take pictures with the new FortiGate router in place (front and back with visible link lights). Technician will then fill out work order and will also have the Home Depot manager sign off on the Customer Release form (attached to this WO).
