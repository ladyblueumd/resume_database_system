Service Description (Any questions, call while on site 877-413-7338)

Work to be done M-F 9AM-5PM 

POS/Merchandising Experience preferred but not required.

Visit the store to swap & install new chiller with store assistance.

Will need to remove the new chiller from the pallet, move from back room to set in place on store floor. Will need to set shelves, price tags and product. Store confirmed they have staff that will be available to help move the chiller.

Materials Required: Tape Measure, Scissors, Ability to print checklist & hold for pick up sign.

Check in with Manager-Ask them for all Freshpet price tags to be printed.

Identify New Chiller in back room

Unpack

With store assistance, remove new chiller from pallet and set in place on store floor. The new unit will be replacing an existing unit. Leave the product in the existing unit until you set up the shelves, transfer the product and take old unit to the back room and put Hold for Pick Up sign on it.

Adjust shelf heights in chiller according to Planogram.

Place product according to Planogram.

Place price tags according to Planogram. Do not peel & stick, cut them out and place them in the plastic channel strips on top of the green channel graphic as per the planogram. Place all price tags even if product is not yet available.

Take picture of completed chiller, serial number, thermostat, and completed chiller in the aisle of the store. Upload on Field Nation and call 877-413-7338 while still on site so any changes can be made while you are still at the store.  *If you don't call while on site, you will be expected to return to the store to make corrections at no additional cost *
