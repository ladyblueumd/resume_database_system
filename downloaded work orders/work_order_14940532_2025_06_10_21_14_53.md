Service Description

A. Summary of Work

Technician will be packing equipment into lockboxes to be securely shipped to 2 different locations. They will need to sort through equipment and separate them by generation 4 and 5 Surface laptops - Surface 4 and 5 laptops will be delivering to Edina and the remaining equipment will be delivering to HTG. Once determined technician will use 'Inventory Pickup Approval Form' to record all system serial numbers, asset tags (if available), and user's names. They will also need to use a label maker to create labels of the serial numbers along with the user's names and place on devices. After devices have been scanned and labeled, place devices into lockboxes. Once all lockboxes have been packed, Technician will need to record lock numbers and seal numbers on the 'Inventory Pickup Approval Form' then will affix seals to lockboxes and lock the boxes.

Please read confidential information section thoroughly as we have included important details!

NOTE: Print Inventory Pickup Approval Form or have access to the excel spreadsheet while onsite

***Label maker and scanner is required for this work order***




B. Tools Needed to Complete Work

Basic tool kits, fully charged laptop, fully charged phone, scanner, label maker, any additional tools if tech needs them.
