Scope Of Work:


Prepare (4) SunPrint Printers to be returned to Leasing Company. 

Here is each step of our process:

​​

For the SunPrint HP Printers w/o Hard Drives

1.      Arrive on time and call point of contact

2.      The point of contact will provide you with an evidence bag, Sharpie permanent marker, and a trash bag

3.      Locate Printer

a.       If you cannot find the printer, call Tamara for instructions

4.      On checklist, check off “Could you locate the printer”

5.      Locate the serial number on the printer

6.      Take a photo of the serial number on the printer and the Asset Tag Number located underneath the bar code (upload to WO)

7.      On checklist, check off “Emailed picture of serial number on the printer”


8.      Remove the toner cartridge

9.  Place toner cartridge in the trash bag

10.  On checklist, check off “Removed toner cartridge”

11.  On a blank sheet of paper, write the name of the city in which the printer is being shipped to

12.  Tape paper to printer

13.  On checklist, check off “Taped city to printer”

14.  Move the printer to the designated shipping area given by point of contact




For the SunPrint HP Printers with Hard Drives

1.      Arrive on time and call point of contact

2.      The point of contact will provide you with an evidence bag, Sharpie permanent marker, and a trash bag

3.      Locate Printer

a.       If you cannot find the printer, call Tamara for instructions

4.      On checklist, check off “Could you locate the printer”

5.      Locate the serial number on the printer

6.      Take a photo of the serial number on the printer  and the Asset Tag Number located underneath the bar code (upload to WO)

7.      On checklist, check off “Emailed picture of serial number on the printer”


8.      Remove the toner cartridge

9.  Place toner cartridge in the trash bag

10.  On checklist, check off “Removed toner cartridge”

11.  Locate the printer Hard Drive (a small Philips, Torx, or flat screw driver will be required)

12.  Disconnect the cable (leave cable and carrier inside printer) and place the Hard Drive in the provided evidence bag (Note: once the bag is sealed it cannot be reopened w/o destroying the bag)  If no Evidence bag is available please  use Manila Envelope and tape shut.

13.  Write the printer serial number, Hard Drive serial number, and your name on the outside of the evidence bag. (More than 1 Hard Drive per bag is OK as long as all are recorded)


14.  Give the evidence bag to the point of contact

15.  Request for the point of contact to sign the evidence bag

16.  Take a photo of the evidence bag with the Hard Drive inside (upload to WO)

17.  On checklist, check off “hand-off hard drive to point of contact”

18.  On a blank sheet of paper, write the name of the city in which the printer is being shipped to

19.  Tape paper to printer

20.  On checklist, check off “Taped city to printer”

21.  Move the printer to the designated shipping area given by point of contact




Required Tools:

1. Screw Driver Set to include Torx bits

2. Laptop with Excel

3. Cell phone or Camera to take photos
