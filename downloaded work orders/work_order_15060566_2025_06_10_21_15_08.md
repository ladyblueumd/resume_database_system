Service Description

We are in need of technicians to assist our client with removing old POS devices and install new iPad devices.

Position: Desktop Technician




Tasks Required: 

The deployment process will include:

Greeting the local site contact and introducing the technician(s)
Familiarizing with the local environment and the location of the equipment to be deployed and the systems/users to be refreshed
Disconnecting old system from appropriate peripherals
Removing old POS systems and bring to a secure area to store for return to Client warehouse
Bringing new equipment to it to the end-user desk
Unboxing the new equipment and setting it up
Connecting new equipment to all appropriate peripherals
Test new system to ensure connectivity, printing, etc.
Provide end-user with brief familiarization with new system
Bring old system and related peripherals/accessories back to staging area
Create a list of old system and peripheral serial numbers and send to Client
Document new equipment location within the store




Equipment to be deployed:

One (1) Dell Optiplex 7010 as a server
Four (4) Fixed iPad registers
The majority of stores have Four (4) registers
The range of registers will be from three (3) to seven (7)
Two (2) Mobile Inventory iPhones
Two (2) Overflow iPad registers




Skills Required:

​3 years of hands on experience and/or equivalent in desktop hardware/software installations, problem diagnosis and repair.
Strong knowledge of Windows.
Strong knowledge of imaging and data migration procedures.
Assist in and resolve PC related issues with the guidance of the Client Help Desk.
Ability to follow Client provided instructions for basic installs, moves, adds and changes.
Make appropriate use of technical documentation. 
Ability to speak and write clearly to ensure accurate communication.
Conduct himself/herself in a courteous, helpful and tactful manner.
Apply a systematic approach to troubleshooting and problem solving.




Tools Required:

Basic screwdriver set.
Basic drill set
Laptop




Attire:

Business casual.
Khaki's or Jeans (no rips, tears and clean).
Polo or collared shirt.
Clean sneakers or dress shoes.
