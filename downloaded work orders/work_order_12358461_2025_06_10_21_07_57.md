Required Skills:

Mid-level to advanced-level technician with experience installing payment devices, specifically Lane 3000 pin pads using FreedomPay software. Technician should be able to follow detailed instructions and be able to troubleshoot if needed.

Field technician shall work in concert with remote software team to convert payment system from Ingenico Lane3000 to new Ingenico Lane3000 devices paired with Freedom Pay software AND STANDS. Technician shall perform inventory of delivered devices, record serial numbers, connect the devices to the POS terminal, work with remote support and onsite management to install software and test devices.  

Tech will be required to call into a Bridgeline upon arrival to site. Tech will need to work with the Bridgeline to get the devices online prior to setting up the stands. The devices can be plugged into the USB ports at the Workstations.

Old Equipment: Pack decommissioned equipment in boxes from new equipment, and drop at FedEx for return to depot. Shipping labels provided in boxes. DO NOT return old stands! Those can be disposed of on site.
