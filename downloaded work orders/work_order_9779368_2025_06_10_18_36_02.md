We are looking to relocate roughly 420 computers/terminals. To do this effectively and efficiently we are looking for approximately 15 techs that can handle the steps needed. A basic set of hand tools will be needed incase monitors need to be disconnected from arms etc…

Take photos of office / desk you are addressing ( they may need to be referred to later )

Assist in packing up equipment into the transport container for safe relocation.

*** take notes of anything needed, items missing, extra devices etc... ***

*** take notes of anything needed, items missing, extra devices etc... ***
